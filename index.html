canvas

Canvas is a student run showcase featuring work from illustration, graphic design, and art direction students. The event will be taking place from 4/26-4/28 and will include an opening show and an alumni panel discussion.

All ComD students are encouraged to participate and will be given designated wall space to showcase their work.

We will ask that you attend one of multiple mandatory information sessions so ensure that communication is clear and the event runs smoothly. More information with dates and times will be sent via email.

There will be free food at the event and every participant will receive a custom screen-printed t-shirt for the event.
