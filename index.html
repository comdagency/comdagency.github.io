canvas

Canvas is a student run showcase featuring work from illustration, graphic design, and art direction students. The event will be taking place during the second semester and will include an opening show and an alumni panel discussion.

All ComD students are encouraged to participate and will be given designated wall space to showcase their work.

Due to all on campus events being cancelled through the end of the academic year, we will be holding a virtual canvas through our instagram page. Students and their work will be featured on the ComD Agency Instagram page.
